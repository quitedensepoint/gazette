Frontlines to see the 57mm M1 in Action !
French frontline units are soon to be pitting the first production runs of the 57mm M1 in action against the enemy. Army High Command visiting Tourcoing to view production progress of the 57mm M1 were said to be eagerly anticipating that battle weary Army units hard pressed in the ongoing war would soon be able to press home a much increased combat flexibilty after the new 57mm M1 reaches the frontlines.
