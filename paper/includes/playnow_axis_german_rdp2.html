German prepares Pak38 for Frontline Duty.
High Command representitives of the German Army speaking from Maastricht .. there to inspect progress of the new Pak38 were quoted as saying production of the Pak38 should be taking precedence over other weapons which will probably be scaled back in an effort to increase battlefield effectiveness of their units at the front. Frontline Army units are anticipating the arrival of this new combat flexibilty for the extra fighting edge it will offer them in the war effort all along the front.
