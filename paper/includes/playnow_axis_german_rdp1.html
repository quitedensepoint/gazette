Germany Alters Production Priorities.
German Army High Command Officials announced today that several gun class combat units will get 4cm Flak28 units in greater numbers by the end of this cycle. Fighting near British Training has slowed enough to allow shipment of these units to the front. Opposing Allied forces were expected to meet this change in enemy initiatives in the coming RD&P cycles, but the significant increase will be a welcome addition to the German Army front line forces.
